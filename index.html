Clients come to the Law Offices of Timothy A. Green for exceptional litigation skills. Mr. Green is an aggressive advocate who will personally litigate your case, and give you the individual attention that you deserve. Mr. Green will explain all facets of the litigation to you and advise you of the strengths and weaknesses of your case. Our firm endeavors to alleviate the worry and frustration that can accompany a lawsuit. We meet one-on-one with clients and make their needs our priority. We offer a Free Telephone Consultation and accept some cases on a contingency fee basis - which means the client pays no attorney's fees unless we are successful.

We have the skills and resources to handle even the most complex cases. Often we are referred cases from other law firms because of our litigation skills and expertise. In fact, other attorneys have retained us to represent them in lawsuits in which they are personally involved!

We are a full service civil litigation firm conveniently located in downtown White Plains, with a focus on matters such as personal injury, matrimonial and family law, contract disputes, real estate disputes, construction disputes, insurance disputes, discrimination, partnership disputes, shareholder disputes, legal malpractice, declaratory judgment actions and Article 78 proceedings, just to name a few.

Our firm represents both plaintiffs and defendants in New York State Supreme Courts in Westchester County, Bronx County, New York County, Rockland County, Kings County, Nassau County, and beyond, as well as the First, Second and Third Judicial Departments of the Appellate Division. We also represent both plaintiffs and defendants in the United States District Courts for the Southern District and Eastern District of New York.
